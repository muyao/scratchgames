INSTRUCTIONS


===== General Controls =====
- Movement: WASD or ↑←↓→
- Reset: R
- Back to menu: Green Flag
Types of jumps:
- High: Press and hold W
- Light: Tap W
- High Hop: Press and hold W while pressing S
- Light Hop: Tap W while pressing S
You move the fastest while hopping, which is useful
for speedrunning...

===== Editor Controls =====
- Activate Flight: E
- Deactivate Flight: T
- Undo: U
- Cancel Platform: O
To add a platform, drag your mouse from one point
to another, then press Enter.
Switch between different platform types using 1 - 5:
- Basic: 1
- Jump-pad: 2
- Goal: 3
- Anti-jump: 4
- Snail: 5
Press and hold S + Enter to save your level. MAKE SURE
YOU COPY THE WHOLE SAVE CODE!
Press and hold L + Enter to load a level to edit. Leave
it blank to cancel.
This is different to the "Load Level" button, which is
not in editor mode.

===== Platform Types =====
- Basic: Just the basic blue platform
- Jump-pad: Launches the player up. Height increases by
10% every bounce.
- Anti-jump: Prevents the player from jumping
- Snail: Slows the player down
